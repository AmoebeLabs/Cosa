CosaTermostat
=============

Demonstration of temperature and humity reading from a DHT11 and
control of heater and fan with output pins.

Circuit
-------
The DHT11 sensor to D7. The relay for heater control on D6 and fan
control on D5 (Arduino ==> Device).

    D5 ==> DHT11 data  
    D6 ==> Relay/heater  
    D7 ==> Relay/fan
